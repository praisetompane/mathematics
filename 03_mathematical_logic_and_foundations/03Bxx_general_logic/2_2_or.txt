def ∨ | or  | disjunction | inclusive disjunction(Kunen, 2007:4) (Weisstein):
    - formal:
        let:
            T := True
            F := False
            ϕ be a proposition
            ψ be a proposition
        then:
            ϕ   ψ   ϕ ∨ ψ
            T   T     T
            T   F     T
            F   T     T
            F   F     F

        remark: We define/choose the meaning of the word 'or' for the Mathematical context.
