def quadratic polynomial:¹ 
    a polynomial of the form:
        ax² + bx + c, where
            a ≠ 0
        with degree = 2

def quadratic equation:² 
    a quadratic polynomial equated to some value.
        standard form: 
            ax² + bx + c = 0
                where a,b,c ∈ ℝ
        Q: What is a standard form of an equation?
    Examples: 
        2x² + 4x + 2 = 0
