addition:
    - plain english: add the Real parts together and the Imaginary parts together.
    - formal:
        (𝑎+𝑏𝑖) + (𝑐+𝑑𝑖) = 𝑎 + 𝑐 + 𝑏𝑖 + 𝑑𝑖 (Censor, 2015).
    - example:
        (1 + 2𝑖) + (3 - 5𝑖)
            = (1 + 3) + (2𝑖 - 5𝑖)
            = 4 + (-3𝑖)
            = 4 - 3𝑖
References:
    Censor, A. Algebra 1M - international. 05 - Complex numbers. 2015. Technion - International School Of Engineering.
