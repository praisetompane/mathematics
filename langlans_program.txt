def Langlands program (Frenkel): 
    "continents" of mathematics²
        * Number Theory: Explains numbers
        * Harmonic Theory: Explains Harmonics/Waves
        * Geometry: Explains the intrinsic shapes of mathematical objects

def symmetry:
    transformations that preserve
        shape and position of an object.
    - examples:
        - sphere: ∞ symmetries
        - square: 90°, 180°, 270°, 360° rotations.
