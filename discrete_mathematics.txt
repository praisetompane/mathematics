def¹ discrete_mathematics: mathematics that describes processes that 
     consist of a sequence of individual steps (Epp, 2010:xiv). 

def² discrete_mathematics: group of mathematical branches or parts of branches that studies non continuous mathematical objects.
     objects that are individually separate and distinct

References:
    Epp, S.S. 2010. Discrete mathematics with applications. 4th ed. Cengage Learning. Boston, MA.
