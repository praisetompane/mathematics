def proof theory (Kunen, 2007:7):
    studies the structure of formal proofs.

def proof (Montalban, 2020):
    an argument
        that uses logical steps
        to show a mathematical statement
        follows from certain assumptions.

    logical steps = rules


References:
    Kunen, K. 2007. The Foundations of Mathematics.
    Montalban, A. Math 125A -- Mathematical Logic. https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLjJhPCaCziSRSUtQiTA_yx5TJ76G_EqUJ.
